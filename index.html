Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

1. Introduction

PhotoRelease ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Our Core Commitment: We collect ZERO data.

PhotoRelease is designed with privacy as its foundation. All photo analysis, duplicate detection, and cleanup operations happen entirely on your device. We never upload, transmit, or access your photos.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your photos or any photo metadata (EXIF, location, timestamps, etc.)
  • Your personal information (name, email, phone number, contacts)
  • Your device information or unique identifiers (IDFA, device ID)
  • Your usage patterns, analytics, or behavioral data
  • Your location data (GPS, IP address)
  • Any other user data whatsoever

3. On-Device Processing

All app functionality operates entirely on your device:

  • Photo Analysis: Uses Apple's Vision framework locally to detect duplicates and analyze quality
  • Duplicate Detection: Processed on-device using perceptual hashing algorithms
  • Live Photo Conversion: Handled by Apple's PhotoKit locally
  • Storage Optimization: All operations use iOS native APIs without network access

Nothing leaves your device. Ever.

4. Third-Party Services

PhotoRelease uses only Apple's native frameworks and NO third-party SDKs:

  • StoreKit 2: For in-app purchases and subscriptions (managed entirely by Apple)
  • PhotoKit: For secure photo library access
  • Vision: For on-device image analysis
  • SwiftData: For local data storage

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or attribution SDKs
  • Crash reporting services (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Any other third-party tracking or data collection tools

5. Purchase Information

When you make a purchase or subscribe:

  • All transaction processing is handled exclusively by Apple through StoreKit 2
  • We only receive a cryptographic receipt validation from Apple to verify your purchase status
  • We never see your payment information, credit card details, billing address, or Apple ID
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device only
  • No purchase data is transmitted to any server

6. Network Activity

Zero Network Requests: PhotoRelease does not make any network connections for any functionality. The app operates completely offline.

How to Verify: You can independently verify our zero-network claim using:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → App Privacy Report
  • Network monitoring tools like Charles Proxy or Wireshark

You should see zero network requests from PhotoRelease.

7. Data Storage

The app stores minimal operational data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Monthly quota: Tracks your free tier usage (50 operations per month)
  • Purchase status: Whether you have an active subscription or lifetime purchase
  • App preferences: Your settings and UI preferences

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not backed up to iCloud.

8. Photo Library Access

PhotoRelease requests access to your photo library only to:

  • Scan for duplicate and similar photos
  • Detect screenshots and low-quality images
  • Identify Live Photos and their storage usage
  • Enable you to delete or convert photos you choose

All photo access is read-only except when you explicitly confirm deletion or conversion. Photos are never uploaded or transmitted.

9. iCloud Integration

PhotoRelease does NOT use iCloud sync for any app data. However, the app can:

  • Detect whether your photos are stored in iCloud (to provide accurate space estimates)
  • Access photos stored in iCloud through Apple's PhotoKit (all processing remains on-device)

We never access your iCloud account or iCloud data outside of the Photos library.

10. Children's Privacy

PhotoRelease does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by design, as we collect zero data.

11. International Users

PhotoRelease is available worldwide. Since all processing happens on your device and we collect no data, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data never leaves your physical device.

12. Your Rights

Under privacy regulations like GDPR and CCPA, you have certain rights regarding your data. However, since PhotoRelease collects zero data:

  • Right to Access: Not applicable (no data to access)
  • Right to Deletion: Not applicable (no data to delete)
  • Right to Portability: Not applicable (no data to export)

All your information remains on your device under your complete control.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the app or App Store update notes

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
